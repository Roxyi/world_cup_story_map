World Cup 2018 Team Story: Iceland

After 12 failed qualification campaigns, Iceland finally made it to the World Cup finals. They are for sure the biggest supurise for Russia World Cup 2018.

Let's follow the map camera to find out who they are and how they made a history.

via GIPHY

Population

Iceland has a population of 348,580 (0.3 million) and an area of 103,000 km2 (40,000 sq mi). -- Wikipedia

It is by far the smallest nation to reach the World Cup finals.

On the map, you may zoom in a bit to see the country labels.

Players

Next, let's get to know more about the players.

Within the Iceland men's national football team, the average age is 27.1, with the youngest one of 21 and the oldest one of 34.

Most Iceland players play for the overseas football clubs.

Historic Moment

On June 16st, 2018, Iceland's first game of the World Cup came against Argentina. Iceland was considered a major underdog but they hold Argentina to 1-1 draw.
